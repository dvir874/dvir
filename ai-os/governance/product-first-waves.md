# Product-First Wave Planning Policy
## רגע לפני AI Company OS | CEO Directive | 2026-06-26 | IMMUTABLE

---

## The Law

**Waves are never organized by pages, components, or routes.**

**Waves are organized around business objectives and customer experiences.**

---

## Every Wave Must Answer These 10 Questions

```
1.  What business problem are we solving?
2.  Which stage of the customer journey are we improving?
3.  Which user emotions are we trying to create?
4.  What measurable business outcome do we expect?
5.  Which screens are affected?
6.  Which components are required?
7.  Which Design Pack is needed?
8.  Which Stitch prompts are required?
9.  Which implementation work follows?
10. How will success be measured after release?
```

---

## Mandatory Wave Order

```
Business Goal
    ↓
Customer Journey
    ↓
Experience Pack
    ↓
Design Pack
    ↓
Implementation Plan
    ↓
Implementation
    ↓
QA
    ↓
Executive Review
    ↓
Release
    ↓
Post Release Review
```

---

## The Mental Model

> "Components and screens are implementation details.
> The Wave itself is defined by the customer experience and the business outcome."

Think like a product company, not a software project.
