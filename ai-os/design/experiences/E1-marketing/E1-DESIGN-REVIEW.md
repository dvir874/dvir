3. **Product Preview section:** Cream background. iPhone device mockup showing actual RSVP screen. Right side: "RSVP שיגרום לאורחים לומר וואו" Frank Ruhl Libre 700. Three benefit bullet points with checkmark icons.

4. **Features 3×2 grid:** 6 equal cards, each with icon + title + one-line description. Features: RSVP / Guests / Seating / WhatsApp / Gallery / Budget. Cream cards, gold icon accent.

5. **Footer:** Dark background, "רגע לפני" logo, nav links.

## Issues to Fix Before Approval

**Issue 1 — Testimonials section missing:**
The brief specified a testimonials section (3 cards with couple names + 5-star rating + specific quotes). Stitch did not generate it. Add in implementation: 3 testimonial cards between features and CTA.

**Issue 2 — Missing CTA section:**
The brief specified a second full-bleed CTA section (candlelit photography + "מוכנים להתחיל?" + gold CTA). This creates a strong page ending. Add in implementation.

**Issue 3 — Registration page not shown:**
The brief included a registration start page. This requires a separate Stitch call.

---

## Strengths (What Stitch Got Right)

**The hero photography choice is correct.** Warm candlelit reception tables — the exact atmosphere the product is managing. Couples who see this image know immediately: "This platform understands my wedding."

**The iPhone mockup showing the actual RSVP** is the right product proof device. It's not a generic "app screenshot" — it shows the exact screen their guests will see. This is the product's best marketing asset.

**The 3×2 feature grid** gives complete product overview without overwhelming. Each feature gets equal weight — no misleading priority.

**The typography system** (Frank Ruhl Libre hero + Heebo body) maintains brand authenticity throughout a long page.

---

## Design Verdict

**APPROVED — with 3 additions (testimonials, closing CTA section, registration page).**

The landing page establishes the correct first impression: warm, premium, Hebrew-first. The photography choice, typography, and product proof are all correct.
