## Screen 1: Post-Event Survey
**Mobile 390px | Warm Thank-You, Not Feedback Form**

### Visual Review
- Olive branch botanical header illustration — matches brand botanical vocabulary
- "תודה שהייתם איתנו ❤️" Frank Ruhl Libre 700 — past tense, grateful
- "חתונת ענבל ונדב" gold muted + "26 ביוני 2026" muted
- Q1 cream card: "איך הייתה החוויה?" — 5 star rating (stars, not numbers)
- Q2 cream card: "מה הרגע שהכי אהבתם?" — 4 radio options
- Q3 cream card: "השאירו ברכה לזוג" — textarea with warm placeholder
- Gold "שלחו ←" full-width CTA

### QA
| Criterion | Score | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Tone | ✅ 10/10 | Thank-you first, questions second — emotional sequence is correct |
| Question Count | ✅ 10/10 | 3 questions only — respectful of guest's time |
| Star Rating | ✅ | Stars more emotional than NPS scale |
| Moment Selection | ✅ | 4 meaningful moments: חופה / ריקוד ראשון / ארוחה / בילוי |
| Blessing Field | ✅ | Third question = personal connection, not an afterthought |
| RTL | ✅ | All text right-aligned, radio buttons right side |

### Verdict: **APPROVED** ✅
The survey honors the guest relationship. Three questions is exactly right — more would feel like work.

## New Patterns Confirmed — E2

**Thank-You Survey Pattern:** 3 questions max. Opens with gratitude (not "please rate us"). Stars not scales. Ends with blessing field — guests leave something, not just give a score.

**Time Capsule Countdown:** "365" in Frank Ruhl Libre 900 gold is the same scale as the couple's days-to-wedding countdown. This visual rhyme is intentional — the capsule is a mirror image of the countdown, reversed in time direction.

**Memory Wall Editorial Mix:** Blessing text cards + photos in a masonry grid. The mix prevents either type from feeling dominant — it is a wall of love, not a photo album.
